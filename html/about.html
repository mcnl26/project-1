About

This website was created to promote the Province of Camarines Norte. It is been a norm that when Bicol region is mention the first thing that comes to mind is Mt. Mayon which is located in Albay. Naga City as somehow seen as the capital city of Bicol. Camarines Sur , Sorsogon and Masbate which been the places for adventurers and beach lovers.

This website aims to put The Province of Camarines Norte in the minds of people when you mention Bicol. Because it also offers the same vibe when you visit the other Bicol provinces. This website emphasize the foods and places that you can experience to feel and remember the province.

In partnership with the Camarines Norte LGU more exciting places will be included to this website that hopes to attract more tourists.
